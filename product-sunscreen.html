Skin Toner

PKR 2,200

Our Skin Toner is a refreshing, balancing formula that completes your cleansing routine. Formulated with natural ingredients and hydrating agents, this toner helps restore your skin's pH balance, tighten pores, remove residual impurities, and prepare your skin for better absorption of serums and moisturizers.

Key Benefits:

  • Restores and balances skin's pH level
  • Tightens and minimizes pores
  • Removes residual dirt and impurities
  • Provides instant hydration
  • Calms and soothes the skin
  • Prepares skin for better product absorption
  • Refreshes and revitalizes tired skin
  • Suitable for all skin types

Key Ingredients:

Rose Water: Natural hydrating and calming properties.

Witch Hazel: Gently tones and tightens pores.

Hyaluronic Acid: Provides instant hydration.

Aloe Vera: Soothes and calms the skin.

Glycerin: Attracts and locks in moisture.

Natural Botanical Extracts: Provide additional benefits and antioxidants.

No Alcohol: Gentle formula that won't dry out your skin.

How to Use:

Step 1: After cleansing your face, pour toner onto a cotton pad or your hands.

Step 2: Gently pat or swipe the toner across your face and neck, avoiding the eye area.

Step 3: Let it absorb for 30 seconds to 1 minute.

Step 4: Follow with your serum and moisturizer.

Alternative Method: You can also spray it directly onto your face and gently pat it in.

Best Used: Morning and evening after cleansing. Can also be used throughout the day as a refreshing mist.
