Hand Serum

Our premium Hand Serum is specially formulated to nourish, repair, and rejuvenate your hands. Enriched with powerful hydrating and brightening ingredients, this serum helps restore softness, reduce dryness, fade age spots, and keep your hands looking youthful and well-cared-for.

Key Benefits:

  • Nourishes and repairs dry, rough hands
  • Brightens and evens hand skin tone
  • Reduces dark spots and age spots on hands
  • Deeply hydrates and softens skin
  • Strengthens nails and cuticles
  • Improves skin elasticity and firmness
  • Protects against environmental damage
  • Provides anti-aging benefits for hands

Key Ingredients:

Vitamin C: Brightens skin and reduces dark spots on hands.

Hyaluronic Acid: Provides intense hydration to dry, rough hands.

Shea Butter: Deeply nourishes and softens hand skin.

Vitamin E: Protects and repairs damaged skin on hands.

Jojoba Oil: Mimics natural oils to restore hand moisture.

Retinol: Helps reduce fine lines and age spots on hands.

Natural Plant Extracts: Provide additional healing and soothing properties.

How to Use:

Step 1: Wash your hands and pat dry.

Step 2: Apply a small amount (about a pea-sized) of hand serum to the back of your hands.

Step 3: Gently massage into hands, fingers, and cuticles in circular motions.

Step 4: Allow it to absorb for 2-3 minutes before touching anything.

Best Used: Morning and evening, or after washing hands. For best results, use at night before bed.

Tip: Apply before wearing gloves for deeper penetration and overnight treatment.
