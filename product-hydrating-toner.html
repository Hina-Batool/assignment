Hydrating Toner

PKR 670

Our original Hydrating Toner is a perfect daily essential that has been part of our collection since the beginning. This gentle yet effective toner hydrates, nourishes, and tones dry skin, preparing it for the rest of your skincare routine.

Key Benefits:

  • Provides instant hydration
  • Nourishes and revitalizes skin
  • Tones and balances dry skin
  • Removes residual impurities
  • Preps skin for better product absorption
  • Calms and soothes irritated skin
  • Lightweight and non-sticky formula
  • Perfect for daily use

Key Ingredients:

Rose Water: Natural hydrating and calming properties.

Witch Hazel: Gently tones and tightens pores.

Glycerin: Attracts and locks in moisture.

Aloe Vera: Soothes and hydrates the skin.

Natural Plant Extracts: Provide nourishment and antioxidants.

Alcohol-Free: Gentle formula that won't dry out your skin.

How to Use:

Step 1: After cleansing your face, pour toner onto a cotton pad.

Step 2: Gently swipe across your face and neck, avoiding the eye area.

Step 3: Let it absorb for 30 seconds.

Step 4: Follow with your serum and moisturizer.

Best Used: Morning and evening after cleansing.
